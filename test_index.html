About Us

BAYSTATE Wire & Cable is a regional supplier servicing Massachusetts and New England for over thirty years. Our Industry knowledge and expertise allows us to effectively meet and exceed our customer’s demands. We carry all major wire & cable brands, but also build our stock around our customer’s specific needs. If we don’t have something on the shelf that you use, let us know and we will stock it for you. If you need a special application wire or cable, we will have it manufactured for you.

We believe that building strong vendor relationships is key to providing our customers with the highest quality product and services at the very best value
